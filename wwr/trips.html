Our trips

Provo River

Enjoy the scenic beauty of the Provo River. Nestled in the Uinta mountains and surrounded by tall pines. You will see the beautiful wildlife Provo has to offer. Our rafters often see hawks, eagles, mountain goats, beavers, and deer. A fan favorite along this route is the famous bridal veil falls. Our tour guides are masters at story telling the history of this lovely region. Book early for this trip. The available time for this run is short because we must wait until the water level is low enough to be safe from the spring runoff and the cold comes early at this elevation.

Colorado River

The Colorado River is among the more famous rivers in the western United States. On this trip you will enjoy the dramatic horseshoe bend. This meandering river is lined by enourmous cliffs on either side. This trip takes you right past some amazing ancient native american dwellings and wall carvings. The many arches, caves and the sandy beaches in the basin make this trip a memorable one.

Green River

Traveling by water is one of the mosts comfortable ways to enjoy Moab's breathtaking desert. Enjoy the incredible canyon walls while laying back, gently riding along on the green river. This is our most popular trip for large families. It's lower intensity is better for a group with a variety of ages. While the water is calm, the scenery is exciting. Towering red rock cliffs and plateaus line this beautiful body of water. The wildlife and plant life is abundant. You are likely to see lizards, hawks, cati, squirrels, mountain goats, deer, sheep.
