Overview

Purpose

It is time to bring our wonderful company and products to the web. After starting ten years ago and developing an exciting ecofriendly line of camping and rafting products, we are finally creating an online store. We plan to grow our revenue and share the lastest technology in rafting equipment. Our forum will help us support newcomers to the community where everyone can share their rafting experiences. And where our employees publish a blog and rate the best destinations for rafting.

Audience

Our audience is Kayakers and rafters. They want to connect with other rafting enthusiasts and where newbies can get advice about equipment, safety and the best destinations. Our audience will most likely be accessing from a laptop or desktop but it would be a good idea to make our articles and forums assessible from mobile devices.
